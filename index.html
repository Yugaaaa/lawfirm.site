Our Expertise

Practice Areas

Comprehensive legal services delivered with precision, discretion, and an unwavering commitment to justice across all domains of law.

Corporate Litigation

Representing corporations and businesses in complex commercial disputes, shareholder disagreements, contractual breaches, and regulatory proceedings before all forums.

Civil Litigation

Expert advocacy in property disputes, injunctions, recovery suits, and civil wrongs. Meticulous preparation of plaints, written statements, and appellate briefs.

Criminal Defense

Vigorous defense in Sessions Courts and High Court Bench, covering bail applications, anticipatory bail, and full trial representation for all categories of offences.

Family Law

Compassionate and strategic counsel in matrimonial matters, divorce proceedings, child custody, maintenance claims, and adoption under applicable personal laws.

Real Estate & Property

Title verification, sale deed drafting, partition suits, encroachment disputes, and registration matters across Coimbatore and western Tamil Nadu districts.

Constitutional & Writ

Filing and arguing writ petitions before the Madurai Bench of the Madras High Court in matters of fundamental rights, service law, and administrative law.

Labour & Employment

Industrial disputes, wrongful termination, ESIC/PF matters, and management-side advisory before Labour Courts and the Industrial Tribunal.

Motor Accident Claims

Comprehensive representation before Motor Accidents Claims Tribunals, securing rightful compensation for victims and their families with thorough case documentation.

Notary & Documentation

Notarial services, legal drafting of agreements, affidavits, power of attorney, and statutory declarations with meticulous attention to legal form.
